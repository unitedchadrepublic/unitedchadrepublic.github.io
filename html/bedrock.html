Bedrock Rock Breaking Service!

Do you have some pesky bedrock that needs to be broken?
Well for the low price of 5 diamonds one of our skilled members will come and break bedrock for you!
Simply follow the instructions below:
  1. Rename 5 diamonds with your IGN and place in the bedrock breaking chest at the Chad Star
  2. Go under the nether roof
  3. Click f3 and find a block that has a target y value of 127
  4. After you find the block save the coords and go to the nether roof
  5. Place a sign on the nether roof indicating which block needs to be broken
  6. Make area is cleared of blocks that might get blown up!
  7. Fill out the form here with the coords of the block in the message
*Please note that it might take hours-days before the order is completed!*
